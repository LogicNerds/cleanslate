Privacy Policy

Clean Slate Audit ("Clean Slate," "we," "us") is a sole proprietorship operating under LogicNerds, based in California. This policy explains what information we collect from you, how we use it, who we share it with, and how you can have it deleted.

1. What We Collect

When you use the Clean Slate portal, we collect only what's needed to build your personalized audit:

  • Uploaded documents — income statements, bank/credit statements, and other financial documents you choose to upload.
  • Questionnaire answers — the information you enter in the portal wizard, including debts, spending categories, and your stated financial goals.
  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (handled securely by our authentication provider; we never see your password in plain text).
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We do not receive or store your card number or full payment details.

We do not link to your bank accounts, and we do not pull any data on an ongoing basis. Everything we have is what you directly upload or enter.

A note on sensitive identifiers: we strongly encourage you to black out or redact Social Security numbers, full account numbers, or other sensitive identifiers before uploading any document. We don't need them to build your audit.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to produce your audit deliverables: a personalized video walkthrough and an interactive Excel workbook. Your documents and answers are reviewed personally by Elijah, the sole operator of Clean Slate, to build these deliverables. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to anyone.

3. Who We Share It With

We use a small number of service providers to operate Clean Slate. Each only has access to what's needed to perform its function:

  • Firebase (Google) — account authentication, document storage, and the database that holds your portal information.
  • Cloudflare — runs backend automation, including account reminder emails.
  • Resend — sends transactional emails (like delivery notifications) on our behalf.
  • Stripe — processes payment for the audit.

Besides Elijah, no one at Clean Slate has access to your uploaded documents or questionnaire answers. We do not share your information with any other third party, and we do not sell your data.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your uploaded documents and questionnaire answers for 30 days after your audit is delivered, in case you have follow-up questions about your results. After 30 days, this data is automatically and permanently deleted.

You don't have to wait: you can permanently delete your files and account at any time using the Request Data Deletion button in your portal Settings. This action is immediate and irreversible — once submitted, your documents, questionnaire answers, and account are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

You can also request deletion by emailing elijah@logicnerds.net.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored using Firebase's authentication and storage infrastructure, with access restricted to your own account and to Elijah as the service operator. No online system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, correct inaccurate information, or delete your data at any time, using the methods described in Section 4. You must be 18 or older to use Clean Slate.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Clean Slate after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to elijah@logicnerds.net.
